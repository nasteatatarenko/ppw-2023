STRAY KIDS EVERYWHERE ALL AROUND THE WORLD

Welcome to the Stayville website. This is a virtual journey into the world of music, which begins with Stray Kids.

  • Stray Kids (스트레이 키즈) is an eight-member boy group under JYP Entertainment. They were formed through the competition reality show of the same name. Originally as nine, they debuted on March 25, 2018 with the mini album I Am Not.
  • Stray Kids' logo is written in Bang Chan's handwriting.
  • Stray Kids are highly involved in the creation of their songs. The lyrics and melodies are theirs and, according to Bang Chan, they are given a lot of freedom.
  • The struggles and thoughts explored in their songs often mirror those of their fans.

Stray Kids is not just music, it's movement, philosophy and art. They gave us several hundred songs, but also caused endless emotions and hopes. They are a bridge between music and life. Join our journey and I assure you it will leave a mark in your hearts and give you faith in your abilities.

You make Stray kids Stay

STAY is the official fandom name for the South Korean boy group Stray Kids."STAY" has two meanings: a place where people who are wandering (Stray) can stay (STAY), and the reason for the existence of Stray Kids, as the letter 'r' in 'Stray' in the group name is interpreted as reason. The fans are the reason why and where Stray Kids can stay.
