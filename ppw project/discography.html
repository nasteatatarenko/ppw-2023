Thanks to Stray kids, you will have the opportunity to discover musical treasures that match your mood, style and inner attitude. Stray Kids is not just a musical group, they are the voice of a new generation. They talk about important social and psychological issues, discuss topics of self-identification, youth uncertainty and explore complex aspects of modern life through their songs. In their voices you will find a song for every taste. On this page you can familiarize yourself with the group's discography.
